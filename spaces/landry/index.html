Welcome Breathen to the home of faith, below are our gathering times, feel free to be a part of our movement

Monday

Prayer Meeting

17H50 - 18H50

Every monday we have a prayer meeting, welcoming the new week and ushering blessing upon it. Join us for every prayer every monday.

Youth Meeting

17H50 - 18H50

Youth you are the future of our society, to all the youth! God is not one who judges, he is love, kindness and patience we urgue to come in any shape or form and join our youth team to help you with your mental and spirtual well being in this these times in the 21st century.

17H50 - 18H50

As men we gather and stand for what we love and belive in, we stand in the gap for our loved ones and pray against any evil that may come between you and prosperity for for your family. Join the men's fellowhip every friday, and lead your family into blessing in abundance.
